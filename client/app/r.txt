Klaimit : Where Creators Own Their Ideas

Show your work. Protect it. Earn from it.

Have someone ever took credit for something you own?, that feeling sucks

Klaimit is a website where you can upload your creative works, like art, photos, or designs and keep full ownership of it. People can see your work, appreciate it, or buy the right to use it but they can’t steal it. It’s safe and simple, all in one click.

How It Works
Klaimit makes ownership simple:
1. Upload your idea, share your art, photo, or concept.
2. We tag it as yours. Klaimit gives your upload a special digital ID that proves you’re the real owner.
3. You decide what to do. Keep it private, share it with the world, or trade it for tokens (like selling your idea, safely).
It’s like turning your creativity into a digital asset, something that can be viewed, admired, or even bought, but never stolen.
What Makes Klaimit Special
* Keeps your work safe and linked to your name.
* Simple for everyone. no tech skills needed to use Klaimit.

* You can earn when people trade or license your ideas.
* Connect with other creative people like you.

Our Goal
We want every creator (artists, designers, writers, and dreamers) to have full control of their ideas.
With Klaimit, your creativity is more than just a post, it’s your property.


Your ideas deserve more than likes, they deserve credit, ownership, and value.
And now, you can finally have all three in one place.
